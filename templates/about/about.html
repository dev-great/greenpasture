THE SPIRIT OF LIFE MINISTRY INTERNATIONAL INCORPORATED

Bishop Samuel D. Ocheja is the presiding Bishop and founder of the Spirit of Life ministry International Incorporated and the senior Pastor of Green Pastures Assembly. He has a vision of reaching the world with the word of God with emphasis on purity and power, prosperity and purpose. He has been in full time preaching and teaching ministry since 1990. He is a graduate of Ahmadu Bello University Zaria with a degree in economics. (1988) and a PhD from Acts seminary and graduate schools. He travels extensively across the nations of the world preaching and teaching in churches, crusades, seminars and conferences and God confirms his word with diverse miracles, healing and deliverances and salvation of souls. He also presides over the spirit of Life Bible College, the training arm of the ministry. He is happily married to pastor Mrs Florence Ocheja and they are blessed with three boys and two girls. They reside in Abuja Nigeria from where they oversee the work of the ministry.

CHRISTIAN EDUCATION

The Spirit of life Bible college is the ministerial training arm of the ministry which was established in 2000.The vision of the Bible college is to train and equip men and women with the word and necessary skills that will enable them to reach the dying world and bring in the harvest of souls as they fulfil the call of God on their life. Experienced ministers are available to impart the knowledge of God in a way to make them relevant in the challenge of the 21st century global Christian ministry. The college offers the following courses;

  • (1). Advanced Bible Course (Certificate course)
  • (2). Ministerial Bible Course. (Diploma course)
  • (3). Bachelor of Theology (Degree course)

All courses are currently being offered on part time basis and lectures are held on weekends. Many have graduated from the college and have gone ahead to serve in various capacities as Christian workers in churches and ministries across the world as agents of change and reaching the lost for Jesus.Admission forms are available at our office address. or for more info call the college administrator on 08032361659: 08057230060.
